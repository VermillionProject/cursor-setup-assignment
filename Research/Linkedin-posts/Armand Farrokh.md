**Summary:**  
Armand Farrokh shares a practical LinkedIn outreach framework designed to increase meeting bookings. He advises against pitching prospects immediately after connecting, as sales-focused connection requests are often ignored or deleted.

Instead, he recommends a two-step approach. First, send a simple or lightly personalized connection request without a pitch. After the connection is accepted, send a message that combines business relevance with personal context while directing the conversation toward email. He also suggests using personalized follow-up messages separately to increase visibility in the prospect's inbox.

Armand emphasizes that successful LinkedIn outreach relies on relevance, personalization, and relationship-building rather than immediate selling.

**Key Insight:**  
The most effective LinkedIn outreach focuses on creating curiosity and relevance first, rather than pitching immediately after connecting.
