**Summary:**  
Kevin Dorsey discusses the growing role of AI in sales and warns against becoming overly dependent on it. While he strongly supports using AI to improve productivity, analyze conversations, and accelerate learning, he argues that many sales professionals are allowing AI to replace critical thinking rather than enhance it.

He explains that top-performing salespeople use AI as a tool to learn faster, identify patterns, and improve decision-making. In contrast, weaker performers often rely on AI to generate answers, messaging, and strategies without fully understanding the underlying principles.

Kevin emphasizes that AI rewards curiosity and learning but can become a crutch when used as a substitute for skill development. He encourages sales professionals to use AI to deepen their understanding rather than outsource their thinking.

**Key Insight:**  
AI should accelerate learning and skill development, not replace critical thinking and professional judgment.
