**Summary:**  
Jason Bay shares his perspective on what separates top-performing sales representatives from average performers after working with thousands of sales professionals across hundreds of organizations. He describes four common sales rep archetypes: the grinder, the procrastinator, the cyborg, and the owner.

According to Jason, the most successful salespeople operate as "owners" of their territory. Rather than focusing solely on activity volume or relying heavily on automation tools, they carefully qualify opportunities, provide strategic value to prospects, and challenge buyers with compelling insights that drive action.

He argues that in an increasingly AI-driven sales environment, the ability to think strategically, build trust, and create meaningful buyer conversations is becoming a key differentiator.

**Key Insight:**  
The highest-performing salespeople act like owners of their territory by prioritizing qualification, value creation, and strategic buyer engagement over pure activity volume.
